Hi there, My name is Temitope and welcome to my learning journal

After several months of self-learning, I took the leap into a structured bootcamp program. This journey has been both challenging and exhilarating as I strive to gain expert guidance and immerse myself in hands-on projects. Each day brings new opportunities to refine my skills and collaborate with like-minded individuals.

How I stay committed to learning

Staying consistent in my learning journey requires discipline and curiosity. I dedicate specific hours each day to coding, starting with small achievable goals. I also experiment with integrating new techniques into existing projects to solidify my understanding.

To avoid burnout, I ensure to take breaks, reflect on my progress, and celebrate small wins. This approach keeps me motivated and helps me maintain a steady pace while acquiring new knowledge.

How I got started

My interest in programming began during high school when I became fascinated with how websites and applications work. Initially, I watched online tutorials and built simple web pages. Over time, I explored structured courses and communities that fueled my enthusiasm for web development.

Today, I continue to build on this foundation, diving deeper into advanced topics and collaborating with peers to tackle exciting projects. Each step in this journey reminds me why I fell in love with coding in the first place.
